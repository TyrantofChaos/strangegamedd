MegaDude

Green Team

MegaDude's Development cycle was exactly 4 weeks or 28 days to be exact. We were not given anymore time than that to create a game loop with Unreal Engine 5 with a start and end state. There were five members of the team, and we all helped in different ways. My job as Team Lead was making progress reports to the producers, GitBash/Github (we had not been shown how to use perforce yet), Jira tickets, reviewing pull requests, and debugging errors.
