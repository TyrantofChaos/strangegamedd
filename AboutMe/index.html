About Me

Hello, you have found Douglas Strange's Portfolio site. I program everything on this site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I spend most of my time in game development — a multidisciplinary programming field that blends technical systems, design logic, and creative problem-solving. My specialties include Unreal Engine 5, Unity, and Game Maker, along with C++, C#, and GML, respectively. In my spare time, I’m currently learning Python, as well as the spoken languages German and Japanese.

I am the Solo Developer of the below projects:

  • "Project Valkyrie" an Unreal Engine 5, RPG inspired by the classic Legend of Heroes franchise
  • "Project Blue" a Unity Engine, tactical RPG inspired by Fire Emblem and the Disgaea series
  • "Project Green" an RPG Maker Engine, smaller scale turn-based RPG than the previous two games with a larger focus on learning design skills.

My favorite games are "Trails of Cold Steel" (the whole series), "Devil May Cry 5," and "Honkai Star Rail" as they have great stories and fun gameplay. I love anime from "Ketekyo Hitman Reborn" and "Bleach" to "The Apothecary Diaries" and "Dan Da Dan." I also love CCGs like "Shadowverse" and "Cardfight Vanguard" for the ability to create game plans and execute them.
