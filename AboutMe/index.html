About Me

Hello, you have found Douglas Strange's Portfolio site. I program everything on this site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I spend most of my time in game development — a multidisciplinary programming field that blends technical systems, design logic, and creative problem-solving. My specialties include Unreal Engine 5, Unity, and Game Maker, along with C++, C#, and GML, respectively. In my spare time, I’m currently learning Python, as well as the spoken languages German and Japanese.

I am the Solo Developer of a project by the tentative title of "Project Valkyrie" an RPG inspired by the classic Legend of Heroes franchise. The game is still very early in development, and so am I. RPGs are my passion and grand stories are what I live for. My goal in life is to create a story as enthralling as Falcom's Legend of Heroes franchise or pre-Arise Bandai Namco Tales series.

My favorite games are "Trails of Cold Steel" (the whole series), "Devil May Cry 5," and "Honkai Star Rail" as they have great stories and fun gameplay. I love anime from "Ketekyo Hitman Reborn" and "Bleach" to "The Apothecary Diaries" and "Dan Da Dan." I also love CCGs like "Shadowverse" and "Cardfight Vanguard" for the ability to create game plans and execute them against opponents attempting their own plans.
